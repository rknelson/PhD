Economic or Environmental?

Many of the tweets that included the words refinery or refineries are very economic focused. Some of the main words that were included the tweets were oil, prices, shut, gas, rise, and texas. From these words, one can infer that the people behind the tweets were focused on the rise of gas prices due to the hurricane.
